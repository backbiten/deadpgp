# Whistleblower Protocol

This document outlines the protocol for whistleblowers within the DeadPGP project. It serves as a guideline for individuals wishing to report misconduct or violations of our principles while ensuring their protection and anonymity.

- Introduction
- Principles of Reporting
- Anonymity and Protection
- Procedures for Reporting
